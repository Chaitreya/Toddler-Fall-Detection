About

The project's goal is to create a toddler-specific fall detection system that is accurate and dependable in order to reduce the risk of catastrophic injuries from falls. The main objective is to develop a machine learning-based fall detection system that can quickly recognise falls based on the motion, posture, and balance of the children and rapidly alert their parents or carers before the fall occurs.
